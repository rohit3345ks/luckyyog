Introduction

Yoga is more than just a form of exercise. It is way to harness and improve the strength and balance of your body and mind, as well as a way to relax.


Principles

Yoga focuses on five main principles, designed to enhance mental and physical well being which includes Relaxation, Exercise, Breath Control, Positive Thinking and meditation etc


Benefits

Studies have shown that people practicing yoga have exprienced dramatic increases in lung capacity, improved ability to handle stress, and reductions in body weight,cholestrol and blood sugar levels.
